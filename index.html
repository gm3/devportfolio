About Me

Hello, my name is Godfrey Meyer, a FullSail University Recording Arts Grad from 06. I have been working in musicn production a number of years, producing music for many TV networks, and tv shows. Some of those credits include MTV, VH1, Comedy Central, BET, Bravo, SyFy, Discovery Channel, Spike, NAT Geo, NBC, NICK, OXEGEN, Sundance, TLC,TRAVEL, and many others! Currently I have been working in Virtual Production, software dev, C#, Unity3d, and creating Virtual Reality Studios, producing and filming music videos inside VR!

Experience

Virtual Reality Development And Production

VR Game Dev and Production

I have created many worlds inside Unity3d, inside VR, for people to use as Production environments for live broadcast. This is a extremely new concept. I am active in VR development and communities inside Social VR. I create and produce 3d content, stages, and production environments for Virtual Production. We use 3d avatars to blog, podcast and create digital media content. Unity3d is a game engine, and we use it to create these words. For instance I created a VR Recording Studio, and a Green Screen Studio, as well as podcast rooms with camera and custom production tools for many productions and music videos filmed INSIDE VR over the network in realtime.

BMI

Songwriter / Composer

Recording Audio Engineer
